Lexical Resource evaluates how well the candidate has used a varied range of vocabulary to express their ideas. For scoring band 5, the candidate has only basic words and there are frequent errors. For scoring band 6, the candidate needs to use some less common vocabulary and there may be some errors, but it can still be understood. For scoring band 7, the candidate needs to show a wide range of vocabulary with no errors, and they should use idiomatic or phrasal expressions appropriately. For scoring band 8, the candidate needs to use a high level of vocabulary that is precise, and with a great range of idiomatic expressions.
